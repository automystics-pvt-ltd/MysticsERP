Design and implement a robust enterprise-grade Approval Workflow Engine as a core platform service for the entire Inventory Management application. The approval framework must be fully configurable, scalable, secure, and reusable across all modules without hardcoded logic. Support dynamic single-level, multi-level, parallel, conditional, and sequential approval workflows for Purchase Orders, GRNs, Supplier Invoices, Supplier Payments, Purchase Returns, Stock Transfers, Stock Write-offs, Inventory Adjustments, Discount Overrides, Price Changes, POS Day Closing, User & Role Changes, Master Data Updates, and all business-critical transactions.

Provide a centralized Approval Configuration module where administrators can define workflow rules, approval hierarchies, approvers, role-based routing, amount-based approvals, warehouse-based approvals, department-based approvals, business conditions, escalation rules, delegation, substitute approvers, approval matrices, auto-approval rules, and exception handling. Support unlimited approval levels and future extensibility without code changes.

Implement complete approval lifecycle management including Draft, Submitted, Pending Approval, Approved, Partially Approved, Rejected, Sent Back, Cancelled, Escalated, and Closed statuses. Include Approve, Reject, Send Back, Reassign, Delegate, Request Changes, Add Comments, Add Attachments, and Approval Notes functionality. Ensure all approval actions are fully audited with timestamps, users, comments, status history, and complete transaction traceability.

Create a centralized Pending Approvals Dashboard with role-based views, approval queues, notifications, SLA monitoring, overdue approvals, approval analytics, workload tracking, and actionable insights. Support in-app notifications, email alerts, reminders, escalations, and approval history tracking. Transactions must be locked or restricted appropriately while under approval and should update inventory, financial, and operational records only according to approved business rules.

Ensure workflows integrate seamlessly with RBAC, audit logging, reporting, dashboards, inventory transactions, financial transactions, and all application modules. Add search, filters, pagination, exports, approval reports, KPI dashboards, and approval analytics. Deliver a modern, responsive, enterprise-grade UI/UX with intuitive approval screens, clear status indicators, workflow visualizations, seamless navigation, mobile compatibility, high performance, and complete data integrity. The solution should be production-ready, highly configurable, secure, scalable, and capable of supporting future business processes without architectural changes.
