Shopify Integration & Product Sync - Enterprise Requirements
1. Simplified Enterprise UI
After Shopify is connected, display only a clean dashboard with key metrics and actions.
Hide technical details (API scopes, webhooks, permissions, etc.) under an Advanced Settings section.
Modern enterprise UI with cards, progress indicators, filters, search, and drill-down reports.
2. Fixed Warehouses
Only 3 system warehouses are allowed:
Main Warehouse
Shopify Warehouse
Store Warehouse
These warehouses cannot be added, edited, or deleted by any user, including Super Admin.
Validate all product mappings against these warehouses.
3. Product Sync Validation
Investigate why product prices differ after sync.
Validate and synchronize:
Selling Price
Compare-at Price (MRP)
Cost Price
Tax
SKU
Barcode
Inventory
Images
Variants
Ensure no data mismatches between ERP and Shopify.

Verify stock before and after every sync.
